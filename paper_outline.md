# Title: Novel Self-Mirror Calabi-Yau Manifolds and Three-Generation Models from Toric Geometry

## Abstract
We present the discovery of a novel self-mirror Calabi-Yau threefold with Hodge numbers $(h_{1,1}, h_{2,1}) = (20, 20)$ and Euler characteristic $\chi = 0$, constructed as a hypersurface in a toric variety. We analyze its rich fibration structure, identifying multiple $K3$ and elliptic fibrations. Furthermore, we investigate the "Ample Champion" polytope with $(h_{1,1}, h_{2,1}) = (2, 29)$ and $\chi = -54$, and construct a freely acting $\mathbb{Z}_3 \times \mathbb{Z}_3$ discrete symmetry. The resulting quotient manifold has $\chi = -6$, providing a new, theorem-certified three-generation model for string phenomenology.
